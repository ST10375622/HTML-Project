How to submit a problem

This page will breifly expalin how to submit an issue if you are having any trouble. If you have any trouble or do not understand how everything works you can use the contact details on the About us page.

  1. You need to take a picture with your phone and store it in a location you will find it.
  2. Next you need to click on the file choose file button. It will then take you to your files and then you need to locate the image.
  3. choose the image.
  4. Then describe the problem as clear as possible in the description.
  5. Then you will need to click on the location button to get the current co-ordinets of your location.
  6. You will then click on submit

After you have submitted the issue it will then be sent to the database.
It will get fixed as soon as possible and an update to your issue will be made.
